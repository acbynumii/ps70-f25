About Me Final Project

Week 1: Intro

For my final project, I plan to design and build a robotic arm inspired by Dum-E from Iron Man. The arm will have multiple joints for precise movement and will be controlled by an Arduino or Raspberry Pi. I will integrate a voice command interface, allowing the robot to respond to spoken instructions like picking up, moving, or releasing objects. To make it more engaging, I will program simple personality features, such as playful responses or light/sound feedback. This project combines mechanical design, electronics, and software to create a functional and interactive robotic assistant.
